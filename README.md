# Strategic Advisory & Business Impact Case Studies

## 📌 Overview
A portfolio of strategic consulting case studies utilizing financial modeling, market research, and risk assessment to solve complex corporate challenges across the technology, healthcare, and retail sectors.

## 📂 Featured Analyses & Methodologies

* **Cloud Platform Financial Benchmarking (AI Infrastructure)** * *Data:* FY2025 Form 10-K SEC filings for Amazon, Microsoft, and NVIDIA.
  * *Methodology:* Financial ratio analysis calculating Net Profit Margin, ROA, ROE, Return on Invested Capital (ROIC), and liquidity metrics (Current/Quick ratios, Collection Periods). 
  * *Analysis:* Diagnosed that Amazon's retail operations mask AWS's true profitability (dragging net margins to 10.8% against NVIDIA's 29.6% ROE). Recommended a formal AWS spin-off study and SKU-level profitability reviews.

* **Financial Management & Sustainable Growth (Moz)** * *Data:* Harvard Business School Case 9-820-002 (historical financials highlighting a $12M cash burn on $29.3M revenue).
  * *Methodology:* Applied Robert C. Higgins' Analysis for Financial Management (AFM) sustainable growth rate formula ($g* = P \times R \times A \times \hat{T}$).
  * *Analysis:* Identified that launching 4 products in 4 years caused actual growth to exceed sustainable growth, leading to extreme cash burn. Recommended pruning marginal products to restore core profitability.

* **Infrastructure & Supply Chain Risk (BEAD Deployment)** * *Data:* Budgetary and workforce data regarding Michigan’s $1.56B broadband expansion initiative.
  * *Methodology:* Mapped over 30 specific job roles directly to core cost centers (allocating 50-60% of budget to Civil Works & Physical Construction vs. 15-20% to Engineering/Soft Costs).
  * *Analysis:* Evaluated the "Allocation Risk" caused by federal "Build America, Buy America" rules, illustrating how a concentrated fiber optic supply chain disproportionately threatens small ISPs.

* **Gig Economy & Market Defensibility (Walmart Spark)** * *Data:* Secondary market research aggregating gig workforce demographics, platform margins (<4% Adjusted EBITDA), and last-mile market projections (>$300B).
  * *Methodology:* Regulatory and competitive risk assessment evaluating the impact of the U.S. DOL's independent contractor guidelines and Prop 22.
  * *Analysis:* Developed operational recommendations to improve driver Net Promoter Score (NPS) and platform defensibility to combat high workforce churn.

* **Technology Risk & Healthcare Compliance (Digital Trust)** * *Data:* Operational profiles for Community Health Systems (CHS), representing 67 hospitals and $13.8B in revenue.
  * *Methodology:* Mapped existing operations against the NIST Zero Trust Architecture (SP 800-207) and NIST AI Risk Management Framework.
  * *Analysis:* Delivered a strategic roadmap for implementing Zero Trust security to ensure HIPAA compliance and establish secure AI diagnostic governance.
